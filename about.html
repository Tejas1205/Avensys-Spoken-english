About Fluent English

We are a dedicated spoken English training institute in Hadapsar, focused on helping individuals become confident English speakers.

Whether you’re starting from zero or polishing your professional English, we have a course for you.

To make spoken English simple, practical, and fun for every learner in Hadapsar and beyond.
